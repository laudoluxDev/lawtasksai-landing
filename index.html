What is LawTasksAI built on?

LawTasksAI is a collection of expert-designed task templates that run through OpenClaw (MCP-compatible AI runtime) or Claude Desktop. The AI executes on your machine — your documents never touch LawTasksAI servers.

  • Works with OpenClaw, Claude Desktop, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible assistant
  • Supports local AI (Ollama) for 100% air-gapped operation
  • Cloud AI (Claude, GPT-4) also supported — subject to provider terms
  • VirusTotal-verified releases

System requirements

LawTasksAI runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux. For local AI, a modern machine with 16+ GB RAM is recommended (M1 Mac or equivalent). For cloud AI, any modern computer works.
