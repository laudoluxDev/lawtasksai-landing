Rule 1.6 & Security

Your client data is protected with local execution, encryption, and ethical AI practices built for legal professionals.

🛡️ We Never See Your Client Data

LawTasksAI never receives your documents. Your files are processed locally on your machine — we don't see them, period.

For document analysis tasks, everything runs on your computer — documents are never transmitted to LawTasksAI servers. For other tasks, we process legal questions (not client files). We do not see or store case documents, client names, or matter details. Ever.

Understanding Privacy: Two Layers

Many attorneys ask: "Is LawTasksAI Rule 1.6 compliant?" The answer requires understanding two separate systems working together:

⚠️ Important: Privacy Has Two Parts

Layer 1: LawTasksAI (our software) never receives your documents. Files stay on your machine.

Layer 2: Your AI Assistant (Claude Desktop, ChatGPT, OpenClaw, etc.) processes your prompts and may send data to its cloud provider.

For full Rule 1.6 compliance, you need both layers to be private. See recommendations below.

Layer 1: LawTasksAI (Our Architecture)

LawTasksAI is designed with attorney confidentiality as the foundation. Here's what we do and don't collect:

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

Document analysis tasks: Run entirely on your machine. Your files never reach LawTasksAI servers.

Server-side tasks: We process your legal questions (e.g., "What is the SOL for fraud in Texas?") but never see client files or case details.

Data retention: Task results are delivered to you and immediately deleted. We don't train AI on your data. We don't sell your information.

What We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, name, and payment details (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Usage Data: Which tasks you run, credit consumption, and aggregate usage patterns
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, app version for debugging and support

What You Transmit to Us

When you use a server-side task (159 of 202 tasks), your device sends:

  • Your legal question or query — e.g., "What is the statute of limitations for negligence in Colorado?"
  • Your license key — to verify your account and deduct credits
  • Task ID — which skill to run

That's it. You are transmitting legal questions, not client files, not case documents, not client names. We process your question, return the result, and discard the query. Nothing is stored.

For local-execution tasks (43 tasks — document analysis, contract review, deposition summaries), nothing is transmitted to LawTasksAI. Your documents stay on your computer. The only transmission is a request to verify your license key.

Layer 2: Your AI Assistant (The Missing Piece)

This is where confidentiality decisions actually happen.

LawTasksAI doesn't work alone — it integrates with an AI assistant like Claude Desktop, ChatGPT, or OpenClaw. When you upload a document or send a prompt:

  1. Your AI assistant receives your prompt/document first
  2. It may send data to its cloud provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.)
  3. The AI assistant calls LawTasksAI to execute the task
  4. Results flow back through the same path

⚠️ Critical Understanding

If you use a cloud AI assistant (Claude Desktop, ChatGPT, etc.), your documents and prompts may be sent to that provider's servers — regardless of LawTasksAI's local execution.

LawTasksAI is designed for privacy, but full Rule 1.6 compliance requires BOTH layers to be private.

Current AI Provider Policies

  • Anthropic (Claude): Does not train on user data, but processes prompts/documents on their servers
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Does not train on API/ChatGPT Plus data (with opt-out), but processes it in the cloud
  • OpenClaw with cloud LLM: Same as above — depends on the LLM provider
  • OpenClaw with local LLM: Everything stays on your machine (full confidentiality)

Rule 1.6 Considerations

Most state bar associations require attorneys using cloud AI to:

  1. Understand the provider's data handling practices
  2. Ensure reasonable security measures are in place
  3. Obtain informed client consent (in many jurisdictions)
  4. Document your due diligence

Architecture Overview

LawTasksAI includes 202 AI-powered legal skills split across two execution models:

🖥️ Local Execution (43 Skills)

Runs entirely on your computer. Documents never leave your machine. Perfect for sensitive client materials and confidential case files.

☁️ Server-Side (159 Skills)

Processed via our secure Cloud Run infrastructure. All data encrypted in transit with TLS 1.2+. Used for non-sensitive tasks and external integrations.

When Your Data Travels

For server-side skills, data is transmitted to our Google Cloud Run backend for processing. This includes:

  • Task inputs you provide (e.g., legal questions, research topics)
  • Generated outputs returned to your device
  • No persistent storage — data is processed and immediately discarded

Encryption & Security

Data in Transit

All API communication uses TLS 1.2 or higher encryption. Data transmitted between your device and our servers is protected with industry-standard encryption protocols.

Data at Rest

Task outputs are delivered directly to your device. Only account information (email, credit balance) is stored in our secure database. We do not see or store case documents, client names, or matter details.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • OpenAI / Anthropic: AI model providers for server-side tasks (zero data retention per their terms)
  • Google Cloud: Infrastructure hosting with enterprise-grade security

Compatibility with Security Tools

Works with Prompt Guard & ClawSec

If you use security skills like Prompt Guard or ClawSec to protect your AI assistant, LawTasksAI works seamlessly alongside them. Our loader skill simply makes secure API calls to retrieve results — it does not modify system files, inject prompts into other skills, or alter your agent's configuration in any way.

Why Server-Side Is Safer

A recent Snyk security analysis of nearly 4,000 publicly available AI skills found that 7.1% contained critical security flaws — including exposed credentials, prompt injection vulnerabilities, and unauthorized code execution.

LawTasksAI avoids these risks entirely:

🛡️ Typical Public Skills

Full skill code runs on your machine. If compromised, it can access your files, inject malicious prompts, or exfiltrate data. 7.1% of public skills have critical flaws.

✅ LawTasksAI Approach

Only a thin loader runs locally — it sends your query and receives a result. The actual skill logic runs on our secured servers, where it can't access your files or system.

🔐 Supply Chain Security

Every LawTasksAI release is scanned by 70+ antivirus engines via VirusTotal before publication. Our loader contains no executable code beyond a simple API call — there is nothing to exploit. Your documents and client data remain completely isolated from our skill logic.

Your Responsibilities

While we provide secure infrastructure, you play a critical role in protecting your data:

  • Use local-execution skills for highly sensitive documents
  • Review task descriptions to understand whether a skill runs locally or server-side
  • Keep your device secure with updated antivirus software and strong passwords
  • Don't share your account — each attorney should have their own login
