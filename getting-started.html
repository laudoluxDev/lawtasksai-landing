What Are AI Legal Skills?

Skills are specialized legal tools, not services — like a calculator for statutes of limitations or a formula in Excel. Each of our 200+ skills is purpose-built for a specific legal task: statute lookups, deadline calculations, rule summaries, and more. You ask a question in plain English, the skill processes it, and you get a structured, cited answer. No prompts to memorize. No complicated setup. Just like Excel doesn't replace your accountant, LawTasksAI doesn't replace your judgment.

You don't need to know which skill to use. Just ask your question — the AI automatically finds and runs the right one.

You type:

"What's the statute of limitations for medical malpractice in Colorado?"


LawTasksAI returns:

✓ Statute: 2 years from discovery

✓ Absolute limit: 3 years from act

✓ Minors exception: until age 8

✓ Citation: C.R.S. § 13-80-102.5

✓ Key cases + recent changes

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this practicing law?
No. LawTasksAI is a software tool — like Westlaw or LexisNexis. It provides legal information and automates research tasks. It does not provide legal advice, represent clients, or create an attorney-client relationship. You, the licensed attorney, apply professional judgment to every result.
Can opposing counsel subpoena my AI queries?
We don't store your queries or results on our servers. Once your request is processed, there's nothing to subpoena. Research queries (like statute lookups) generally don't contain privileged information anyway — they're questions about the law, not about your clients.
How accurate are the results?
LawTasksAI skills are designed by a former trial attorney with 21 years of practice experience. They cite specific statutes, cases, and rules. That said, AI can make mistakes — always verify critical information, just as you would with any research tool. Think of it as a very fast first draft of your research.
What if I have confidential documents to analyze?
Document analysis skills run locally on your computer. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third party. The AI processes everything on your machine, so attorney-client privilege is maintained.
Do credits expire?
No. Buy credits when you need them, use them whenever you want. No subscriptions, no monthly fees, no pressure.
What platforms does this work with?
LawTasksAI works with Claude Desktop, OpenClaw, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible client. We're adding more platforms regularly. If your preferred tool supports MCP (Model Context Protocol), it works with LawTasksAI.
I'm not technical. Can I still use this?
Absolutely. If you can install an app on your computer and type a question, you can use LawTasksAI. Our most popular platform (Claude Desktop) works just like a chat app. No coding, no command line, no technical knowledge required.
What jurisdictions do you cover?
All 50 U.S. states plus federal. Skills cover statutes of limitations, court rules, filing deadlines, and procedures for every jurisdiction. We're expanding to additional practice areas regularly.
