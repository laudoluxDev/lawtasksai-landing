What Are AI Legal Tasks?

LawTasksAI provides specialized legal tools, not services — like a calculator for statutes of limitations or a formula in Excel. Each of our 200+ tasks is purpose-built for a specific legal function: statute lookups, deadline calculations, rule summaries, and more. You ask a question in plain English, the task processes it, and you get a structured, cited answer. No prompts to memorize. No complicated setup. Just like Excel doesn't replace your accountant, LawTasksAI doesn't replace your judgment.

You don't need to know which task to use. Just ask your question — the AI automatically finds and runs the right one.

You type:

"What's the statute of limitations for medical malpractice in Colorado?"


LawTasksAI returns:

✓ Statute: 2 years from discovery

✓ Absolute limit: 3 years from act

✓ Minors exception: until age 8

✓ Citation: C.R.S. § 13-80-102.5

✓ Key cases + recent changes

Quick Start: Get Your Tasks

Here's the fastest path from zero to running your first legal research query. Follow each step exactly — we'll walk you through everything.

1

Buy a Credit Pack

Start with the Starter pack ($19 for 25 tasks). Each task costs one credit. Credits never expire.

2

Get Your License Key

After purchase, you'll receive a license key by email. It looks like: lt_abc123... — keep this handy, you'll need it in a few minutes.

3

Install OpenClaw

Visit openclaw.ai and follow their install instructions. OpenClaw is the AI assistant that runs LawTasksAI's legal tools.

Once installed, OpenClaw gives you a chat window where you type messages — similar to ChatGPT or iMessage. That chat window is where you'll do everything below.

4

Download the LawTasksAI Skill File

Go to your LawTasksAI Account Page, log in with your license key, and click "Download Skill Loader."

This downloads a small file to your computer (usually to your Downloads folder). You don't need to open or edit this file — you just need to know where it was saved.

5

Install the Skill — Just Tell Your AI to Do It

This is the best part about having an AI assistant — you don't need to figure out the technical stuff yourself. Just tell it what you want in plain English.

✨ Open your OpenClaw chat and type this:

I just downloaded the LawTasksAI skill file to my Downloads folder. Please find it, unzip it if needed, and install it so I can use it. My license key is lt_XXXXX

📝 Replace lt_XXXXX with your actual license key from the email you received after purchase.

Your AI assistant will:

  • ✓ Find the downloaded file on your computer
  • ✓ Unzip it if it's compressed
  • ✓ Move it to the correct folder
  • ✓ Configure your license key
  • ✓ Confirm when everything is ready

That's it. Your AI handles the installation. Skip to Step 6! 🎉

If your AI assistant has any trouble finding the file, just tell it where you saved it. For example: "It's on my Desktop" or "I saved it to my Documents folder."

🔧 Prefer to do it manually? Click here for step-by-step instructions

🖥️ Windows:

  1. Open File Explorer (the folder icon on your taskbar)
  2. Go to your Downloads folder and find the lawtasksai file
  3. If it's a .zip file, right-click → "Extract All" → click Extract
  4. Click the address bar at the top of File Explorer, type this, and press Enter:
    %USERPROFILE%\.openclaw\skills
  5. If the skills folder doesn't exist, create it (right-click → New → Folder → name it skills)
  6. Copy the lawtasksai folder from Downloads and paste it into the skills folder

🍎 Mac:

  1. If the download is a .zip file, double-click it to unzip
  2. Open Terminal (Command + Space → type Terminal → Enter)
  3. Paste this and press Enter:
    cp -r ~/Downloads/lawtasksai ~/.openclaw/skills/

After copying, restart OpenClaw so it picks up the new skill.

6

Enter Your License Key

After the skill installs, OpenClaw will ask for your license key. Paste the key from your email (the one that starts with lt_). You only need to do this once.

7

Ask Your First Question!

Open your OpenClaw chat window and type a legal question in plain English. Try one of these:

  • "What's the statute of limitations for breach of contract in Texas?"
  • "What are the filing deadlines for a personal injury case in California?"
  • "Summarize Colorado's rules on expert witness disclosures."

You don't need to know which task to use — LawTasksAI automatically picks the right one based on your question.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this practicing law?
No. LawTasksAI is a software tool — like Westlaw or LexisNexis. It provides legal information and automates research tasks. It does not provide legal advice, represent clients, or create an attorney-client relationship. You, the licensed attorney, apply professional judgment to every result.
Can opposing counsel subpoena my AI queries?
We don't store your queries or results on our servers. Once your request is processed, there's nothing to subpoena. Research queries (like statute lookups) generally don't contain privileged information anyway — they're questions about the law, not about your clients.
How accurate are the results?
LawTasksAI tasks are designed by a former trial attorney with 21 years of practice experience. They cite specific statutes, cases, and rules. That said, AI can make mistakes — always verify critical information, just as you would with any research tool. Think of it as a very fast first draft of your research.
What if I have confidential documents to analyze?
Document analysis tasks run locally on your computer. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third party. The AI processes everything on your machine, so attorney-client privilege is maintained.
Do credits expire?
No. Buy credits when you need them, use them whenever you want. No subscriptions, no monthly fees, no pressure.
What platforms does this work with?
LawTasksAI works with Claude Desktop, OpenClaw, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible client. We're adding more platforms regularly.
I'm not technical. Can I still use this?
Absolutely. If you can install an app on your computer and type a question, you can use LawTasksAI. Our most popular platform (Claude Desktop) works just like a chat app. No coding, no command line, no technical knowledge required.
Can I use my license key on multiple computers?
Yes. Your license key works on any device — your office desktop, laptop, home computer, wherever. Credits are tied to your account, not a specific machine.
What jurisdictions do you cover?
All 50 U.S. states plus federal. Tasks cover statutes of limitations, court rules, filing deadlines, and procedures for every jurisdiction. We're expanding to additional practice areas regularly.
