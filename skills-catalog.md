# LawTasksAI Skills Catalog

## 1. Contract & Transactional

1. **Clause Comparer** — Compares contract clauses against a firm-standard library.
2. **Lease-Crawler** — Scans hundreds of leases for expiration or renewal dates.
3. **NDA Analyzer** — Flags unfavorable "non-compete" or "indemnity" terms in standard NDAs.
4. **Change-of-Control Monitor** — Scans data rooms for contracts triggered by an acquisition.
5. **Entity Validator** — Cross-references contract parties against Secretary of State records.
6. **Force Majeure Auditor** — Identifies which contracts cover specific events (e.g., pandemics).
7. **Signature Tracker** — Verifies all signature pages and dates are present in a closing set.
8. **Amendment Synthesizer** — Merges original contracts with multiple amendments into a single 'restated' view.
9. **Exclusivity Mapper** — Identifies overlapping geographic or product exclusivity in retail leases.
10. **Termination Notice Generator** — Drafts notice letters based on specific contract breach clauses.
11. **Due Diligence Checklist** — Generates customized DD checklists by deal type.
12. **Representations & Warranties Tracker** — Monitors R&W survival periods and claim deadlines.
13. **Escrow Release Calculator** — Tracks escrow milestones and calculates release amounts.
14. **Assignment Consent Finder** — Identifies contracts requiring consent for assignment.
15. **Governing Law Mapper** — Catalogs governing law and venue provisions across a contract portfolio.
